GOAT Boba

In 1996, Eric Chou was born in Taiwan. Enamoured by the art of milk tea milking, he went on an epic quest to master the art of making it. He would create the first GOAT Boba in 2014. By 2016, GOAT Boba was the most popular new boba shop in Taipei. In 2024, we opened our first shop outside Taiwan. We are quickly expanding and invite you to see what the hype is about!
